The interdependence of the "how" (Science) and the "why" (Philosophy).

Activity: Tri-Layered Analysis

Group work: Identify the three types of claims (Fact, Opinion, Philosophy) within two major dilemmas.

Case Study 1: Climate Change

The problem of global warming demands action, but the path is tangled with scientific evidence, political views, and moral responsibility.

1. Factual/Scientific Truth

  • Atmospheric CO2 levels are 420 ppm.
  • Global average temperature has risen by 1.1¬∞C since pre-industrial times.
  • Melting glaciers contribute X volume to sea-level rise.

2. Philosophical Truth

  • Humanity has a moral duty to preserve the environment for future generations.
  • Wealthier nations bear a greater ethical responsibility for pollution.
  • The intrinsic value of a non-human life form.

3. Opinion/Belief

  • Carbon taxes are the most effective policy for solving the crisis.
  • Climate change is the fault of the previous generation.
  • It is too late to make a difference now.

Case Study 2: Social Media and Free Speech

When a platform bans a user or removes content, is it protecting the community or infringing on rights? The dilemma involves technology, law, and fundamental values.

1. Factual/Scientific Truth

  • The platform's algorithm promotes content based on engagement metrics.
  • The number of users banned for hate speech last quarter was X.
  • Studies show a correlation between platform use and depression levels.

2. Philosophical Truth

  • Defining the moral limits of "harm" in online speech.
  • The concept of a public square versus a private company's property rights.
  • The relationship between truth and liberty (is free speech absolute?).

3. Opinion/Belief

  • The platform's CEO is personally biased against one political side.
  • More regulation is always bad for innovation.
  • The user who was banned definitely deserved it.

Stage 3: Individual Synthesis Writing

SLO: Formulate a coherent conclusion based on the analysis.

Writing Prompt

Based on your group's analysis of Case Study 1 (Climate Change) or Case Study 2 (Social Media), write a short conclusion.

Your Task: 3-5 Sentence Conclusion

  • State which of the three types of claims (Fact, Opinion, or Philosophy) was most critical in framing the dilemma.
  • Justify your choice by explaining how the other two categories are influenced by the one you selected.
